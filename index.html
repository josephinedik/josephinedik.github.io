Josephine Dik

About

I am a doctoral student in the Theory and Logic group at the Technical University of Vienna, under supervision of Agata Ciabattoni. Here, I am part of the LogiCS-COFUND doctoral programme.

My research interest lies in non-classical logics with an application to artificial intelligence, philosophy and legal reasoning, in particular: deontic logic, preference logic and description logic.
